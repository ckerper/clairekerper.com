I offer gallery-grade prints of my existing drawings, as well as extra prints of any custom piece you commission. The pricing is the same either way; once a drawing has been created and scanned, prints are simply a flat fee based on size.

The printing company I work with offers an incredibly wide array of print sizes, from 4x4" to 40x60". Above are the most commonly requested sizes. Reach out if you would like to ask about a different size.

How to Order

If you're commissioning a new custom drawing:

Let me know the number and size(s) of extra prints you would like at any time before I have shipped your finished drawing.

For prints of an existing drawing:

Check out my Etsy shop for the stock of prints I have on hand.

If the size or subject you want is not listed, please contact me by email, Instagram DM, or the "contact shop owner" button on my Etsy page.
