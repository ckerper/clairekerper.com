How to Redeem a Gift Certificate

1. Check out my portfolio

You can see my featured works on the drawings page of this site, and videos of the creation process on my Instagram: @claire.kerper.portraits.

2. Submit a request and reference photos

How it works:

  • You submit the request form.
  • I reach out to you via email to:
    • Provide information about your position in my waitlist (currently about 6 months) and
    • Pin down further details of your portrait.
  • On the agreed-upon timeline, I will create your requested portrait in charcoal, then scan the drawing at 1200 dpi and partner with a professional fine art printing company to provide you with a full size gallery-grade print of the portrait.

What makes a good reference photo?

  • High resolution: Can the texture and direction of the fur be seen clearly?
  • Even lighting: Is any part of the face obscured by shadows?
  • Full subject in frame: Are all ears, paws, and tails within the edges of the image?

If you're not sure whether an image would make a good reference, I'm happy to take a look and provide advice!

3. Follow along as your portrait takes shape

Once I begin work on your portrait, I will provide real-time progress updates to you through email, text, and/or social media (@claire.kerper.portraits on Instagram).
