How to Request a Portrait

Note: If you have a gift certificate, see the redeem a gift certificate page.

1. Check out my portfolio

You can see my featured works on the drawings page of this site, and videos of the creation process on my Instagram: @claire.kerper.portraits.

2. Choose your size

How it works: I create your requested portrait in charcoal (typically takes 2-3 months depending on size), then scan the drawing at 1200 dpi and partner with a professional fine art printing company to provide you with a gallery-grade print at the drawing's original size.

Prints are easy to transport, frame, and preserve, avoiding the fragility of charcoal on paper.

If you prefer the original drawing instead of a print, you can select that as an upgrade.

3. Submit a request and reference photos

After you submit the request form, I will reach out via email to provide information about my waitlist (currently about 6 months) and pin down further details of your portrait. You may submit reference photos with the request form or later on.

What makes a good reference photo?

  • High resolution: Can the texture and direction of the fur be seen clearly?
  • Even lighting: Is any part of the face obscured by shadows?
  • Full subject in frame: Are all ears, paws, and tails within the edges of the image?

If you're not sure whether an image would make a good reference, I'm happy to take a look and provide advice!

4. Follow along as your portrait takes shape

Once I begin work on your portrait, I will provide real-time progress updates to you through email or text.

If the drawing is not intended as a surprise gift, I will also post updates to my Instagram story (@claire.kerper.portraits).

5. Pay & receive your artwork

Payment is due in full on the day of shipment (or in-person delivery). I accept Venmo, Zelle, cash, and check.

6. Leave a review

Let me know what you think! You might end up featured on my testimonials page.
